Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

Miksik Labs operates the Checker mobile application. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

Data Stored Locally

Checker is designed with your privacy in mind. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

  • Work Hours Data: Hours worked, start/end times, breaks, and notes.
  • Company Information: Names and associated hourly rates.
  • App Preferences: Your selected settings.

No Data Collection

Checker does not collect any personal data. We do not:

  • Collect or store your work hours data on our servers.
  • Track your location.
  • Access your contacts or photos.
  • Use analytics or tracking tools.
  • Share information with third parties.

Security

Checker supports Face ID and Touch ID. This biometric data is processed entirely by your device's secure enclave and is never accessed by our app.
